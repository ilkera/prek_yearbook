# Pre-K Year Book

Save your child's **Lillio** (HiMama) daycare photos and videos to your own Mac — and turn them into a beautiful, searchable **Year Book** you can keep forever.

> **Unofficial tool.** Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Lillio (HiMama) or any school or daycare. Made by a parent. Provided "as is," with no warranty.

---

## Download

### ⬇️ [**Download Pre-K Year Book (.dmg)**](https://github.com/ilkera/prek_yearbook/releases/download/v1.0.0/Pre-K.Year.Book.dmg)

*(313 MB — macOS, Apple Silicon. That's the only file you need.)*

## What you need
- A **Mac with Apple Silicon** (M1 or newer) running **macOS 12 or later**
- An **internet connection**
- Your normal **Lillio login**

There's nothing else to install — everything the app needs is bundled inside it.

## Install
1. Open the downloaded `.dmg` and **drag “Pre-K Year Book” into Applications**.
2. **First launch only:** right‑click (or Control‑click) the app → **Open** → **Open**.
   *(macOS shows a warning because the app isn't signed by Apple; this one‑time step tells it you trust the app.)*

## Use
1. Read and accept the terms.
2. Enter your **child's first name** and click **Start**.
3. A browser window opens — **log into Lillio** there.
4. Choose **Photos / Videos** and which **years** you want (you'll see the sizes first).
5. Click **Download**, then **Open Year Book** when it finishes.

Everything is saved to a folder on your **Desktop**: `‹Name›'s Pre-K Year Book/`, with your `photos/`, `videos/`, and a double‑click **`index.html`** Year Book you can browse, search, and share.

## Your privacy
- Your Lillio **password is typed only on Lillio's own page** — this app never sees, stores, or sends it.
- **Everything stays on your computer.** Nothing is uploaded to the author or anyone else.
- The sign‑in uses a temporary browser profile that is **wiped after each run**.
